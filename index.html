How to Play WorldGuessr

Ready to test your geography knowledge? Here's how to play WorldGuessr:

  • You'll be shown an image, map, or street view of a location somewhere in the world
  • Study the visual clues carefully to determine where the location might be
  • Make your guess by clicking or tapping on the world map
  • The closer your guess is to the actual location, the more points you earn
  • Try to reach the highest score by guessing accurately across multiple rounds
  • Challenge yourself with different difficulty levels and game modes

Frequently Asked Questions

What is WorldGuessr?

WorldGuessr is an engaging geography game that tests your knowledge of the world map. Players must identify countries, cities, and famous landmarks based on visual clues provided in the game.

How do I play WorldGuessr?
